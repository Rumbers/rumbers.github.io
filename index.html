Experience

A typical project for us starts with loose requirements - sometimes just a page or two of functional shortfalls in an existing system or a manual process that needs to be automated.

From there we meet the stakeholders, project managers, potential users and involved teams to build up an idea of the new system (or modifications) required. Usually these discussions help to build further documents, slideshows, designs or diagrams. We make a prototype or wireframe of a functional channel and adapt, implement and extend the functionality over multiple internal agile releases until all requirements are satisfied, including those that make themselves known during the process. Then we release, observe and improve according to time and business constraints.
